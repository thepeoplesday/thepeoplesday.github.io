On Saturday May 12, 2018 in community garden in East Harlem, there will be a free-form gathering called The People’s Day. On this day artists, activists, neighborhood institutions and East Harlem community members will come together to share stories and visions for the neighborhood as it faces a new challenge; the implementation of a new rezoning plan.

The People’s Day is free and open to everyone.

The People’s Day is the result of a 2 year research process on Social Engagement Art and Performance by the 111! Collective, a group of 4 graduate students from the Performance and Interactive Media Arts program at Brooklyn College; Alonso Gorozpe, David Rios, Miguel Angel Valderrama and Sabine Blaizin.
